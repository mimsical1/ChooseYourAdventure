An Excellent Choice.

You give in to your gut feeling and follow the little frog. It hops along the street for a minute or two, and before you know it, it is leading you down an alley. Suddenly, you are faced with a large, blue door that seems to have appeared out of thin air. You swear you've never seen it, despite passing through here before.

As you stare at the doors, you hear a small item drop at your feet. You look down and see the little frog dropping a key at your shoe. Its large eyes stare up at you, as if bidding you to pick up the key and enter the mysterious door. However, you are aware of the fact you have a shift to get to, and the clock is ticking. What do you do?

Obey the frog. Go to work.
