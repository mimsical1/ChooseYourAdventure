Feeling Adventurous, Are We...

You pick up the key the frog handed you. You don't know if you imagine it, but you swear the frog smirks at your choice. Hesitant but curious, you unlock the door and enter. You don't know what you were expecting, but it wasn't the forest that you just stepped into. As you look around, you notice the frog hop over to a tree. From its branches hang small, orange fruits.

The frog eyes the fruits, and his eyes seem to sparkle. He looks at you. As you look back at him, you realize he must want you to pick these fruits for him, as he cannot reach them himself. So, you pick the fruits. What do you do with them?

Eat the berries. Give the frog the berries.
